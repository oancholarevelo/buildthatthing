Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All the tools in the "Build That Thing" suite are client-side, which means they run directly in your web browser. Your data is never sent to or stored on our servers. What you create stays on your computer.

Are these tools really free to use?

Yes, 100%. There are no hidden costs, subscriptions, or premium features. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone, without ads or the need to sign up.

Do I need to install any software?

No installation is required. As long as you have a modern web browser, you can access and use all our tools from any device, anywhere.

What file formats are supported?

The Invoice and Resume builders export directly to PDF. The Image Converter currently supports converting PNG and JPG files to the modern WebP format.
